River Clean-Up

Author: Shayne Pierce

Fish Haven City, Natural Resources Manager, Thomas Gardner has initiated a new program titled, “Clean River, Clean Conscience”. This program targets the pollution that occasionally builds up in the local Ninemile Creek as water levels rise in the Spring runoff. Gardner will host a conservation activity each month on the first Wednesday at the Bear Lake Valley boat ramp near the Skinner Cemetery. Volunteers will pick up trash and remove obstructions from the waterway so that water free flow can be achieved before summer busy season. Please wear rubber boots and gloves. For more information call the Natural Resource Management office at 208-555-6543.
