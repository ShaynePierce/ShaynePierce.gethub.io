Site Name:

Perfect 73

I chose "Perfect 73" becuase I like the weather a bit cooler and consider that to be the perfect temperature. Fall time is my favorite. I've heard that some places are a constant 72 or 73 degrees all year long.

Site Purpose:

On this site, our users can, of course, find the current weather and forecast in our covered cities. But also, they can see local activities that would appeal to those who love being outdoors.

Domain:

perfect73.com

The domain I have chosen is "perfect73.com" which was available. I have registered this domain in anticipation of hosting the final project there.

Logo:
