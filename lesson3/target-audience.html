Description:

Perfect 73

Target users are typcially retired senior citizens who appreciate the moderate temperature of 73 degrees. They want to be up-to-date on the weather and 10-day outlook. They want to find a variety of wholesome outdoor activities to plan their week around.

Persona 1:

Arnold

Arnold retired 3 years ago and lives most of the year in Mesa, AZ. He recently puchased a summer home in Preston Idaho. He loves the Preston Rodeo and hiking in the area. He the slower Idaho culture and beauty of the landscape there.

Persona 2:

Janice

Janice is a widow who likes to socialize in the southern Idaho area near her home in Soda Springs. Knowing the current weather is important to her because she is afraid to drive in snow. Seeing activities coming up in the surounding cities has helped her meet many new poeple.

Scenario 1:

Sunbird

It is late April and already the Mesa, AZ temperatures are approaching 100 degrees. Arnold is hot. His air conditioner is constantly running. He looks forward to making the annual trip to Preston. He checks Perfect73 for the weather forcast. He notices the rodeo is earlier this year. He sees several activities and discusses them with his wife.

Scenario 2:

A Stroll

Janice has scheduled an appointment to have some mechanical work on her car. She is curious what activities are happening in Soda Springs that she could walk to while the car is being fixed. She checks Perfect73 and finds an art show is happening in the park. The weather is perfect for a stroll around town.
