Break the COVID-19 Blues - Get out!

Author: Shayne Pierce

While news outlets are constantly blasting the seriousness of the recent global Corona Virus pandemic, including all its depressing statistics of contagions, exposure cases, contamination, and morbidity, the masses are feeling quite down and miserable. This is a perfect reason to get out of the house. Turn off social media and the news. Discover nature and bask in some sunlight. Get some fresh air. All these activities will boost your spirits, give you extra immune response, and keep your chances of contracting any virus very low. Being in sunlight helps your body build up critical vitamin D, which is a front-line to fighting any virus. Don't wallow in the depressing news of COVID. Get out and enjoy yourself and take your family and friends with you.
